Community Edition (open source)

Nexplane's Community Edition will be released as open source under AGPL-3.0 — free to use, self-host, inspect, and modify. AGPL is a strong copyleft license: run a modified version as a network service and you share your changes under the same license. Fine for most self-hosting teams.

Commercial license
